Furniture Refurbishing

Furniture Refurbishing

The Spotless Sweepers are bringing something new into the mix with their cleaning services: furniture refurbishing. Here at the Spotless Sweepers, we're dedicated to saving you money by refurbishing your old furniture into new and improved.

What do we Offer?

Depending on your appointment needs, you can choose weekly, biweekly, or monthly cleaning services.


• Dusting the furniture
• Removing cobwebs
• Scrubbing the insides and under areas that are harder to reach
• Polishing the furniture with sandpaper
• Scrubbing stovetops and drip pans
• Disinfecting the furniture
• Vacuuming (if possible) the furniture
• Custom Paint Finishes (Contact Us)
• Upholstering Furniture
• Minor Furniture Repairs
• Eco-Friendly Refurbishing Options
• Custom Hardware Options (Contact Us)

If there's anything else you need, just let us know!
