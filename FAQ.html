FAQ - The Spotless Sweepers

What are the Booking Appointment Times?

Currently, we are working at 9 AM - 5 PM on Mondays to Fridays, however, we are currently working on Saturdays as well. We are also closed during major holidays, and we'll let you know accordingly.

How do I Pay for my Appointment?

We are a local and small business, so we prefer to handle the money businesses after the appointment has ended. We accept cash or credit card. We calculate the amount of money needed for an appointment based on two things: amount of bathrooms and amount of bedrooms. Bedrooms include kitchens, living rooms, and any other rooms. For window cleaning, each window is $5 to clean and mop. For our bathroom cleaning service, each bathroom is $13 to clean. For furniture refurbishing, the prices vary because of the size of the furniture, however a general price is about $35 for all of your furniture. For the full house package, each bedroom is $20 to clean and each bathroom is $10 to clean with window cleaning, furniture refurbishing, and a car clean for free NOTE: PREORDERED APPOINTMENTS ARE COMING in FEBRUARY 2023.

Why Us?

We're a local business. Our appointments guarantee satisfaction, and if you're not happy with us, we can reimburse your money or clean potentially more. We're also working with eco-friendly products to keep our environment safe and healthy, so you can count on us to keep the enivronment safe. We're also very affordable, and we know how hard it can be to find a reliable, cheap cleaning service, so we decided to be that business. Our locality with Circle C also helps us in keeping in touch with our customers very well to satisfy their needs.

What is your Cancellation Policy?

In order to cancel a service, you must directly contact us through our Contact Us Page, and explain your details as well as your Service ID, that you recieve upon submitting an appointent. In order for your cancellation to be received, you must email us at least 3 days before the service is supposed to take place. Otherwise, there are only special circumstances, where the cancellation request will be accepted.

To Book my First Appointment, How Much in Advance Should I Book the Date?

We recommend that whatever your day you're submitting the appointment request, your date should be at least a week later, or else there could be problems in approving your request as well as the cancellation policies.
