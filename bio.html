About Us

Welcome to my website! My name is Ahantya and I created this local business along with my brother's help to offer helpful, and quick cleaning services in Circle C.

When I moved in at 6 years old, my parents were taking care of me while managaing their lives with my 2 year old brother. It was definitely a problem for them. So what they did do? They hired people to help. As I've grown up, I've noticed the lack of proper cleaning services in the area, so I started researching the Internet, and as I noticed, there weren't many options to pick from.

So I decided that I could step up in my community and create a wider, more accesible cleaning service for my community called the Spotless Sweepers.
