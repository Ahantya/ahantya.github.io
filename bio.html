About Us

Welcome to my website! My name is Ahantya and I created this local business along with my brother's help to offer helpful, and quick cleaning services in Circle C. Circle C is a lively community in South Austin that is dedicated to catering to everybody. It's the perfect place for a family to settle down, per my first hand experience.

To describe my backstory with Circle C, when I moved to the community at 6 years old, my parents were taking care of me while managaing their lives with my 2 year old brother. It was definitely a problem for them. So what they did do? They hired people to help. As I've grown up, I've noticed the lack of proper cleaning services in the area, so I started researching the Internet, and as I noticed, there weren't many options to pick from.

So I decided that I could step up in my community and create a wider, more accesible cleaning service for my community called the Spotless Sweepers.
