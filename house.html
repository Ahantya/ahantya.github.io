What do we Offer?

Depending on your appointment needs, you can choose weekly, biweekly, or monthly house cleaning services.

Some of our cleaning services for house cleaning are:

• Dusting lightbulbs and ceilings
• Dusting and Polishing all the furniture
• Scrubbing and Mopping the Floors and Stairs
• Scrubbing and Disinfecting all Bathrooms including Sinks
• Cleaning Dust and Cobwebs
• Disinfecting the furniture
• Vacuuming Carpets
• Wiping all Windows
• Disinfecting Living Rooms, Kitchens, Closets, Shelves, Fans, and more
