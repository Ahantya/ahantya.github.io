Furniture Refurbishing in Circle C

The Spotless Sweepers are bringing something new into the mix with their cleaning services: furniture refurbishing. Here at the Spotless Sweepers, we know that not everyone wants to buy new furniture and have to get rid of their old ones, so we're dedicated to saving you money by refurbishing your old furniture into new and improved.

What do we Offer?

Depending on your appointment needs, you can choose weekly, biweekly, or monthly cleaning services.

Some of our cleaning services for furniture refurbishing are:

• Dusting the furniture
• Removing cobwebs
• Scrubbing the insides and under areas that are harder to reach
• Polishing the furniture with sandpaper
• Scrubbing stovetops and drip pans
• Disinfecting the furniture
• Vacuuming (if possible) the furniture

If there's anything else you need, just let us know!

Why should you choose our Furniture Refurbishing Service?

Not only are we committed to make your time and money valuable, but we care about the customer's needs. If you aren't satisfied with our service, feel free to let us know what you were expecting, and we will reimburse you.

We have also spent time recently on our products and what we use to clean with. We've made sure that our products are eco friendly, while also trying to find the best material yet.
