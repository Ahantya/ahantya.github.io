Meet the Team!

Ahantya Sharma - CEO




Ahantya is a dedicated member and CEO of the Spotless Sweepers. As he grew up in Circle C with his little brother, he realized that growing a family required a lot of help. A noticable example was cleaning the house because of messes made by little children. So, when he was old enough, he created a business called the Spotless Sweepers along with his brothers. Outside of the Spotless Sweepers, he enjoys playing golf, watching anime, playing video games, and spending time with his family.


Avyukt Sharma - Manager




Avyukt, Ahantya's brother, is the manager of the Spotless Sweepers. He makes sure that the website is up and running and that the business is doing well. He usually enjoys working with others in his free time and is really creative when he wants to be. Though, he's only 13, he is good at managing because of his quick learning abilities and his experience that he learned as he was younger. Outside of the Spotless Sweepers, he likes to play tennis, video games, and explore technology.


Joe Sharma - Advisor




Joe, the brothers' cousins, is the advisor for the Spotless Sweepers. If there's any problems between the brothers or the business that the others don't know how to handle, Joe is able to help with his experience of his father who handles a business. Although his job seems to be easier than the other two, he is highly needed as the Spotless Sweepers is only a local business. Outside of the Spotless Sweepers, he enjoys painting, collecting stones, sleeping, and hanging out with his friends.


Our company, Spotless Sweepers, is a local business that was founded by Ahantya Sharma with help from his cousin and his brother. Our job is to help families in Circle C who are struggling with settling down as we know how hard it can be. Specifically, we strive to help with out cleaning services, and we guarantee that our services are satisfactory. (If they aren't, please contact us, and we can reimburse you.
