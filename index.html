Welcome to the Spotless Sweepers. 

We are located in Circle C where we offer cleaning services for low prices that are affordable.

There are multiple reasons! :

Convenient online booking

We understand that you are busy and don't have time to waste. That's why we've made it easy for you to schedule an appointment online, at a time that is convenient for you.

Simply visit our website and book your cleaning service in a few simple steps. Our team will take care of the rest, ensuring that your home or business is sparkling clean when you need it most.

Customized cleaning plans

Every customer has different needs, and we understand that. That's why we offer a range of cleaning plans to suit your unique requirements. Whether you need a one-time deep clean, or a regular maintenance service, we have a plan that is right for you.

Eco-friendly products

We care about the environment and use only eco-friendly products to ensure that our cleaning services are safe for you and the planet. Our products are biodegradable and non-toxic, so you can trust that your home or business is being cleaned in a sustainable way.
